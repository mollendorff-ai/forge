## What Is Forge? (No Tech Talk)

You know how every company uses Excel spreadsheets for their budgets, forecasts, and financial planning?

**The Problem:**
- Excel files are a mess to manage
- You can't easily track changes (who changed what, when)
- AI tools like ChatGPT can't read Excel files properly
- Big companies pay HUGE money for tools to fix this

**Our Solution:**
Forge lets companies write their financial models as simple text files. Then:
- The computer can read them
- AI can understand them
- Changes are tracked automatically
- It still exports to Excel (so CFOs get their spreadsheets)

Think of it like this: **We built a translator between "human financial planning" and "computer/AI understanding."**
